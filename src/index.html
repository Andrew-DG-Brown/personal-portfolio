About Me

My technical journey as a Full Stack Developer has been marked by a strong command of technologies like JavaScript/TypeScript , Python, Node.js, PostgreSQL, AWS, and front-end frameworks such as Angular and React. At Hubio Technology, I’ve honed my skills in developing dynamic data-driven applications, leading front-end development, and crafting efficient ETL pipelines. My work has directly contributed to significant revenue increases and the streamlining of data processes.

I thrive in environments that challenge me to lead and mentor. Whether it’s driving the front-end development of a multi-tenant application or improving website performance for an e-commerce platform, I’m always looking for ways to push the boundaries and deliver exceptional results. I’m a firm believer in the power of teamwork and AGILE methodologies, as evidenced by my successful project management experiences.

I’m excited to continue my journey, creating impactful digital experiences and sharing my knowledge with the next generation of developers. Let’s build something great together.
